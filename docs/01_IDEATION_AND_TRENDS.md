## 2. Primary evidence

### 2.1 Steam hall-of-famers
- *Stardew Valley*: solo dev, tens of millions of copies, continuous free updates.
- 2026 is being called a peak cozy year — Witchbrook, The Witch's Bakery, Coral Island 1.0.

### 2.2 Cozy + dark twist sub-genre rising
- *Grave Seasons* (cozy farm + hidden serial killer) is hotly anticipated — validates our gentle mystery layer.

### 2.3 Reddit chatter (r/CozyGamers, r/gamedev, r/Stardew, r/IndieGaming)
- 'Witch farming sim' = requested-but-undersupplied combo.
- Demand for shorter polished cozy experiences (12-25 hours) over 100-hour grinds — fits our 6-mission structure.
- Strong interest in stylized 3D cozy (vs. pixel art) — less crowded on Steam.

### 2.4 Discord game-dev communities
- 'Cozy Game Dev' Discord servers report active publisher hunts for cozy pitches in 2026.
